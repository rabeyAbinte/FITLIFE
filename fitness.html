Fitness Tips

Simple and beginner-friendly exercises for students

Daily Fitness Guidelines

Daily Exercise

Spend at least 30 minutes on physical activities like walking, jogging, or cycling.

Warm Up & Stretching

Always warm up and stretch before workouts to prevent muscle injuries.

Stay Hydrated

Drink enough water before and after exercise to stay hydrated.

Beginner Workout Tips

Home Workouts

Simple bodyweight exercises can be done at home without equipment.

Proper Rest

Ensure proper rest and sleep to help muscles recover.

Be Consistent

Consistency is key. Start slow and gradually increase intensity.
